Quickstart Guide
====================
1 Download and unzip AspNetSpell to a convenient location on your hard disk.
2 Add the ASPNetSpell.dll to your Visual Studio toolbox.   You can double click Installer.vscontent to do this. 
3 Drag the directory called "AspNetSpellInclude" to the root folder of your website project.   
4 Drag the SpellTextBox or SpellButton into your Web Page in Visual Studio


* Troubleshooting Guide: http://www.aspnetspell.com/Troubleshooting
* Support: http://aspnetspell.com/Support if you need help with anything!
